plants

Plants are among the living organisms found on the earth, as most of them cover the outer surface of the earth's crust. Plants live in all environments and make their own food. The number of plant species is estimated at more than eight million species. Plants are divided into vascular plants, seed plants, and ferns. And similar to ferns, as plants exist in two types: Spontaneous plants, that is, wild plants that come out without human intervention, and cultivated plants, that is, that which man has cultivated, and the forms of plants are found in the form of trees, herbs, flowers, weeds, shrubs and ferns

Factors affecting plant growth

Many factors influence the growth of plants, namely: The plant's genotype. Environmental factors, i.e. water, temperature, light and soil nutrients. Biotic factors, so that plants compete with others for water, location, light, and food, and there are plants that depend on insects and birds in the pollination process.

The length of the life cycle of plants

Plants have a lifespan like other living things, some plants have short life, others are long, and they organize the life of plants according to their seasonal growth pattern, as follows: Annual plants, so that they grow, reproduce, and live during only one season. Squint plants, which live for two seasons, i.e. grow and multiply twice a year. Perennial plants, which live more than several seasons, so that their reproduction continues after their maturity. Advantages of plants Plants have some characteristics, which are: They are living creatures. It has a true kernel. Their cells have a cell wall, made of cellulose. It is characterized by being immobile. It makes its own food, because it contains chloroplasts. Their bodies are multicellular. Plants live in a variety of environments, meaning they live in water, on land, and in salt water.
