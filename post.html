Carnivorous Plants

Predatory plants are called carnivorous plants, because they feed on the meat of small vertebrate and invertebrate organisms....

Growth conditions of predatory plants

Carnivorous plants need certain conditions in order to be successful in their cultivation and grow well, and they are as follows: Light: carnivorous plants prefer bright light, especially direct sunlight, and cold-temperature lights such as fluorescent help the growth of this type of plants, especially in closed places. Humidity: The growth of predatory plants requires high humidity, as they can be grown in ponds or in swamps in areas with high humidity. Water: rainwater, water, water, and pure water for irrigation.
