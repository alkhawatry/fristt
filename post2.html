Introduction

Introduction Many people think that the importance of plants is limited to being food, a source of oxygen and wood. This is not true at all, because the importance of plants goes beyond this. One of the oldest types of treatments known to the world is herbal remedies, and some believe that this is nonsense and old myths. They rethink the matter, according to recent studies and depending on people's personal experiences, that herbal remedy is real and effective, and it may even be the best.,

The importance of medicinal plants

Since the time people traded a group of herbs with the intention of treating and benefiting from the healing of them, some of them are boiled and the other is eaten, some of them are mixed with other ingredients, and there are many methods that are followed to extract their benefits.

  1. Chamomile: Chamomile flower is useful in treating skin infections, and inflammation of both nails, sinuses, stomach, and liver.
  2. Sage: it is an effective treatment for the effects of premenstruation, and analgesic for stomach pain
  3. Anise: Anise benefits the stomach, expels gas, eliminates bloating, and treats its inflammation.
  4. Peppermint: It has a pleasant aromatic smell, and it is a pain reliever, and a sedative for the stomach,
  5. Fenugreek: Useful for fattening and anemia, for chest diseases such as asthma and cough
  6. Thyme: a food that strengthens immunity, is an antibiotic for bacteria and viruses alike, and protects against influenza and colds.
